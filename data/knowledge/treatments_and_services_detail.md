# Detailed Salon Treatments & Services Guide

## Haircut & Styling Consultation
At SalonAI, every haircut begins with a personalized face shape and hair texture consultation. Our stylists are trained in advanced geometry techniques to complement round, oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.
- **Women's Styling**: Includes relaxing hair wash, blow-dry, and professional styling advice using premium Kérastase and Oribe products.
- **Men's Grooming**: Custom beard trims, fade styling, and scalp massage.
- **Styling Advice**: We recommend trimming your hair every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain shape and prevent split ends.

## Multi-Step Luxury Hair Spa
Our Hair Spa is a restorative treatment recommended for dry, damaged, or color-treated hair.
- **Procedure**:
  1. **Purifying Cleansing**: Washing with a clarifying shampoo to open hair cuticles.
  2. **Nourishing Mask**: Application of an intense hydration mask blended with nourishing oils.
  3. **Scalp Massage**: A 15-minute blood-circulation-boosting head massage.
  4. **Gentle Steaming**: Steam treatment to facilitate deep penetration of nutrients.
  5. **Cool Rinse & Serum**: Rinsing with cool water to lock the cuticles and applying a protective gloss serum.
- **Benefits**: Strengthens roots, controls frizz, reduces dandruff, and deep conditions hair shafts.

## Advanced Keratin Treatment
Our premium Keratin Treatment uses formaldehyde-free formulations (GK Hair and Brazilian Blowout) to restore natural keratin proteins in the hair.
- **Ideal For**: Extremely frizzy, curly, or unmanageable hair. Lasts between 3 to 5 months.
- **Aftercare Instructions**:
  1. Use only sulfate-free and paraben-free shampoos and conditioners.
  2. Avoid washing or wetting the hair for the first 48 hours after treatment.
  3. Avoid tying the hair or using tight hair clips for the first 3 days to prevent creasing.
