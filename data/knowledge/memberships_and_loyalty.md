## Hygiene, Safety & Sanitation Protocols
We follow strict international medical-grade sanitation guidelines:
- **Tool Sterilization**: All metal tools (scissors, clippers, tweezers) are sterilized in an autoclave after each client.
- **Single-Use Items**: Capes, towels, and sheets are freshly washed. Disposables (nail files, buffers) are discarded after a single session.
- **Air Quality**: The salon is equipped with HEPA air filters to capture allergens and dust.
- **Staff Health**: All staff members undergo daily temperature checks and wear protective masks upon guest request.
